"The Whisper" is a personal academic project launched by a student from the National Higher School of Cybersecurity (ENSC). It aims to collect and organize study resources, tools, and notes in one place, focusing on subjects such as algorithms, computer networks, and mathematics. The primary purpose of this website is to help students save time, quickly access important content, and improve their academic performance through simplicity and clarity.
