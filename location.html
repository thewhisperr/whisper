🌟 About The Project

"The Whisper" هو مشروع أكاديمي شخصي أطلقه طالب من المدرسة الوطنية العليا للأمن السيبراني (ENSC)، يهدف إلى جمع وتنظيم الموارد والأدوات والملاحظات الدراسية في مكان واحد، مع التركيز على مواد مثل الخوارزميات، شبكات الحاسوب، والرياضيات. الغاية الأساسية من هذا الموقع هي مساعدة الطلبة على توفير الوقت، الوصول السريع إلى المحتوى المهم، وتحسين مستواهم الدراسي من خلال البساطة والوضوح.
"The Whisper" is a personal academic project launched by a student from the National Higher School of Cybersecurity (ENSC). It aims to collect and organize study resources, tools, and notes in one place, focusing on subjects such as algorithms, computer networks, and mathematics. The primary purpose of this website is to help students save time, quickly access important content, and improve their academic performance through simplicity and clarity.

🎯 Mission & Vision

Welcome to my study resource hub! I'm Whisper, a cybersecurity student who understands the challenge of finding reliable study materials and managing your time effectively. As someone navigating the complex world of cybersecurity education, I created this website to help fellow students discover quality learning resources, organize their study schedules, and streamline their academic journey.

Whether you're looking for comprehensive tutorials, practice labs, certification guides, or time management tools, this platform brings together carefully curated resources to support your educational goals. My mission is to make studying more efficient and accessible, helping you focus on what matters most—mastering the skills that will advance your career in cybersecurity and beyond.
